    ESG

    Sustainability & Governance

    Delta Capital integrates environmental, social, and governance considerations into our investment strategies and operational practices. We recognize that sustainable business practices and robust governance enhance long-term value creation.

    Structure

    Corporate Governance

    Delta Capital operates under rigorous governance standards designed to ensure accountability, transparency, and effective decision-making. Our governance framework reflects the highest standards of institutional management.

    • Board Oversight — Decisions are subject to multi-level review and approval processes that prevent unilateral action and ensure diverse perspectives.
    • Risk Management Committee — A dedicated committee oversees risk policies, monitors exposures, and ensures compliance with established risk limits.
    • Compliance Office — Our compliance function operates independently and reports directly to senior management on regulatory and ethical matters.
    • Internal Controls — Comprehensive systems ensure operational integrity, accurate reporting, and prevention of fraud or misconduct.
    Environment

    Environmental Responsibility

    We recognize our responsibility to minimize environmental impact and support climate-related transition efforts. While our primary focus is investment management rather than physical production, we maintain commitments to environmental stewardship.

    • Climate-Aware Analysis — We integrate climate risk assessment into our research processes and investment decisions.
    • Energy Efficiency — Our operations maintain energy-efficient practices and renewable energy utilization where feasible.
    • Carbon Offset Programs — We participate in verified carbon offset initiatives to address our operational footprint.
    • Sustainable Investing — We offer strategies that incorporate ESG criteria for environmentally conscious investors.
    People

    Social Responsibility

    We are committed to treating people with respect and dignity. Our social responsibility extends from our team to our clients to the broader communities we serve.

    • Diverse Talent — We actively recruit diverse talent and foster an inclusive workplace where all team members can thrive.
    • Fair Compensation — We provide competitive compensation and comprehensive benefits that reflect market standards.
    • Professional Development — We invest in continuous learning and professional development for our team members.
    • Community Engagement — We support community initiatives and charitable organizations aligned with our values.
    • Anti-Corruption — We maintain strict policies against corruption, bribery, and unethical business practices.
    Implementation

    ESG Integration & Measurement

    Research Integration

    ESG factors are systematically incorporated into our quantitative models and fundamental analysis to identify risks and opportunities.

    Performance Metrics

    We track key sustainability metrics including carbon intensity, diversity ratios, governance scores, and ESG ratings across our portfolio.

    Investor Communication

    We provide transparent reporting on ESG metrics and our approach to sustainability to institutional investors and stakeholders.

    Continuous Improvement

    Our ESG framework is regularly reviewed and updated as methodologies evolve and new information becomes available.
