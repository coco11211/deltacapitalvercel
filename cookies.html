    Cookie Policy

    What Are Cookies?

    Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a website. They contain small amounts of information and are commonly used to recognize returning visitors, remember preferences, and understand how people use websites. Cookies are essential for modern websites to function effectively.

    How We Use Cookies

    Delta Capital uses cookies in limited categories to improve your experience and ensure our website functions properly. We are committed to transparency about our cookie practices.

    Categories of Cookies We Use:

    • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for website functionality and cannot be disabled. They enable core features like navigation, access to secure areas, and maintaining your session. Without these, the website cannot function properly.
    • Performance Cookies: These help us understand how visitors use our site, which pages are most popular, and help diagnose technical issues. They do not identify individual users and are used only for aggregated analytics.
    • Preference Cookies: These remember your choices such as language preferences, theme settings, or display options to personalize your experience on future visits.
    • Security Cookies: These help protect against fraud, maintain security on our platform, and prevent unauthorized access to sensitive data.

    Third-Party Cookies

    We limit third-party cookies strictly. Our website uses third-party services only for essential functionality such as:

    • Font delivery (Google Fonts)
    • Infrastructure and hosting services
    • Security and fraud prevention

    We do NOT use cookies for tracking, advertising, behavioral targeting, or analytics that identify individual users. We do not sell or share cookie data with third parties for marketing purposes.

    Your Cookie Preferences

    Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically find cookie settings under "Preferences" or "Settings" in your browser menu. Note that disabling essential cookies may prevent the website from functioning properly.

    How to Manage Cookies:

    • Google Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
    • Mozilla Firefox: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
    • Apple Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
    • Microsoft Edge: Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Cookies and other site data

    Cookie Retention

    Essential and security cookies are retained as long as necessary for their purpose. Performance and preference cookies are typically retained for a limited period and automatically expire. You can clear your cookies at any time through your browser settings.

    Do Not Track

    We respect Do Not Track (DNT) signals from your browser. When DNT is enabled, we limit our use of tracking and performance cookies accordingly.

    Changes to This Cookie Policy

    We may update this cookie policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any material changes will be noted by updating the date at the bottom of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.

    Questions About Cookies?

    If you have questions about our cookie practices or need assistance managing your cookie preferences, please contact us.
