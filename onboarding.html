Employee Onboarding  ·  Internal Use Only

Welcome to the team.

This guide walks you through accessing every tool we use day-to-day. Follow each section in order. If anything is unclear or not working, reach out to your manager directly before trying to troubleshoot on your own.

01

Email

Purelymail
1

Check email in the browser

You can access your inbox from any browser at purelymail.com. Log in with your full work email and the temporary password provided by your manager. Change your password after first login.

Username yourname@deltacapitaltrading.com
Password Provided by your manager
2

Set up on iPhone / iPad (iOS Mail)

Go to Settings → Mail → Accounts → Add Account → Other → Add Mail Account. Enter your name, full email address, password, and a description (e.g. "Delta Capital"). When prompted for server settings, use the values below. Note: username and password for the outgoing server are not optional — fill them in.

3

Set up on Android (Gmail app or any IMAP app)

In the Gmail app go to Settings → Add account → Other and select Personal (IMAP). Alternatively use K-9 Mail or FairEmail — any app that supports IMAP works. Use the server settings below.

4

5

Two-factor authentication

If you have 2FA enabled on your Purelymail account, you must generate an App Password and use that instead of your regular password when configuring mail apps. Log in to purelymail.com to generate one.

All company communication goes through your @deltacapitaltrading.com address. Use it exclusively for work. Webmail is always available at purelymail.com if you need browser access from any device.
02

Slack

Communications
1

Download Slack

Download the Slack desktop app at slack.com/downloads. Also install the mobile app. Use the apps — not the browser.

2

Accept your invitation

An invitation to the Delta Capital workspace will be sent to your work email. Click Accept Invite to join. You do not need to search for the workspace manually.

3

Set up your profile

Add your full name, job title, and a professional profile photo. This is how the rest of the team identifies you.

4

Join your channels

Your manager will add you to the relevant channels. Start with #general and #announcements. Do not create channels without approval.

Slack is for internal use only. Do not share workspace links, credentials, or channel contents externally. All communications are confidential.
03

Notion

Documentation
1

Create your account

Go to notion.so and sign up using your @deltacapitaltrading.com email. Do not use a personal address.

2

Accept the workspace invitation

An invitation will be sent to your work email. Click Accept Invite to join the Delta Capital workspace and access internal documentation, meeting notes, templates, and project tracking.

3

Download the app

Get the desktop app at notion.so/desktop and the mobile app from the App Store or Google Play.

4

Start with the Start Here page

The Start Here page is pinned in the workspace sidebar. Read it before anything else. Do not edit or delete pages you did not create without permission.

Notion is our knowledge base. Meeting notes, documentation, templates, project tracking, audits, and bookkeeping all live here. Treat it as the source of truth.
04

Jira

Task Management
1

Create an Atlassian account

Go to id.atlassian.com and create an account using your @deltacapitaltrading.com email address.

2

Accept the Jira invitation

An invitation to the Delta Capital Jira project will be sent to your work email. Accept it to access the board.

3

Review your assigned tasks

Go to My Work to see tasks assigned to you. Do not reassign or close tasks without confirmation from your manager.

4

Understand the workflow

Tasks move through: To Do → In Progress → In Review → Done. Update your task status as you work. Ask your manager before starting any task that is unclear.

Jira is how work gets tracked. Every task, every assignment, every deadline lives here. Keep your board current — an accurate Jira is a functional team.
