Welcome to the team.

This guide walks you through accessing every tool we use day-to-day. Follow each section in order. If anything is unclear or broken, reach out to your manager directly.

01

Email

Mail
1

Open the Mail app

On macOS, open Mail (pre-installed). On iPhone or iPad, open the built-in Mail app. We use Mail exclusively — no third-party clients.

2

Add your account

Go to Settings → Mail → Accounts → Add Account (iOS) or Mail → Settings → Accounts → + (macOS). Select Other when prompted for account type.

3

Enter your credentials

Your work email address follows the format below. Your temporary password will be provided separately by your manager.

Email yourname@deltacapitaltrading.com
Password Provided by your manager
4

Change your password

You will be prompted to set a new password on first login. Use a strong, unique password. Do not reuse passwords across accounts.

Why Mail? We use the native Mail app across all devices. It is secure, integrated, and requires no additional software. All company communications go through your @deltacapitaltrading.com address.
02

Slack

Communications
1

Download Slack

Download the Slack desktop app at slack.com/downloads. Also install it on your phone. Use the apps — not the browser.

2

Sign in to the workspace

Open Slack and click Sign in to a workspace. You will receive an invitation link to your work email — click it to join the Delta Capital workspace automatically.

3

Set up your profile

Add your full name, job title, and a profile photo. This is how the rest of the team identifies you. Keep it professional.

4

Join your channels

You will be added to the relevant channels by your manager. Check #general and #announcements first. Do not create new channels without approval.

Slack is for internal use only. Do not share workspace links, credentials, or channel contents externally. All company communications are considered confidential.
03

Notion

Documentation
1

Create a Notion account

Go to notion.so and sign up using your @deltacapitaltrading.com email address. Do not use a personal email.

2

Accept the workspace invitation

An invitation will be sent to your work email. Click Accept Invite to join the Delta Capital workspace. This gives you access to internal documentation, meeting notes, templates, and project tracking.

3

Download the app

Download the Notion desktop app at notion.so/desktop and the mobile app from the App Store or Google Play.

4

Familiarize yourself with the workspace

Start with the Start Here page pinned in the sidebar. Do not edit or delete pages you did not create. All documentation edits should be intentional and accurate.

Notion is our knowledge base. Meeting notes, documentation, templates, project tracking, audits, and bookkeeping all live here. Treat it as the source of truth.
04

Jira

Task Management
1

Create an Atlassian account

Go to id.atlassian.com and create an account using your @deltacapitaltrading.com email address.

2

Accept the Jira invitation

An invitation to the Delta Capital Jira project will be sent to your work email. Accept it to gain access to the board.

3

Review your assigned tasks

Once in, go to My Work to see any tasks already assigned to you. Tasks are assigned by your manager. Do not reassign or close tasks without confirmation.

4

Understand the workflow

Tasks move through: To Do → In Progress → In Review → Done. Update your task status as you work. Log time if applicable. Ask your manager if anything is unclear before starting.

Jira is how work gets tracked. Every task, every assignment, every deadline lives here. Keep your board current — an accurate Jira is a functional team.
