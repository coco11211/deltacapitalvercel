    About

    We are building a
    quantitative trading firm.

    Delta Capital is a team of four working to build a systematic trading operation from scratch. We are in the early stages — researching strategies, studying markets, and laying the groundwork for the infrastructure that will power everything.

    We are not live yet. We have not raised capital. We are doing the work first, because we believe the only way to build something lasting is to build it right.

    The Problem

    Separating signal from noise

    Markets generate enormous amounts of data. Most of it is noise. The challenge — and the opportunity — is building systems that can reliably distinguish the two. That is what we are working toward.

    Principles

    Evidence Over Intuition

    We do not guess. Every decision will be grounded in data, tested rigorously, and held to a high standard of proof.

    Risk First

    We think about what can go wrong before we think about what can go right. Survival comes before performance.

    Patience

    We would rather move slowly and get it right than rush to market with something half-built.

    Why

    Why we are doing this

    We believe that systematic, evidence-based approaches to markets can outperform discretionary decision-making over the long run. Not because machines are smarter than people, but because they are more disciplined.

    We are building Delta Capital to prove that thesis — not with borrowed conviction or recycled ideas, but with original research, rigorous testing, and infrastructure we build ourselves.
