    Common Questions

    What does Delta Capital do?

    We are building quantitative trading systems. Our team is developing models that generate systematic investment signals from statistical analysis of market data. We are currently in our development and research phase.

    What markets will you trade?

    We are targeting liquid equities and equity derivatives across major global exchanges. We focus on markets with sufficient depth and transparency to support systematic execution.

    What stage is the company in?

    We are in active development — building infrastructure, conducting research, and validating our models. We are focused on getting the foundation right before going live.

    How are you approaching risk?

    Programmatically. We are designing risk limits to be enforced by the system, not by human judgment. Position limits, concentration limits, drawdown triggers, and exposure caps are being built into every layer of our architecture.

    Will you accept outside capital?

    Not at this stage. We are focused on building and validating our systems. If you are interested in following our progress, you can reach us through the contact page.

    What technology do you use?

    We optimize for reliability and reproducibility over speed. Our systems are being designed to be deterministic from the ground up. We do not disclose specific technology choices.

    Are you hiring?

    Not formally at this time. If you believe you should be on our radar, send a brief note through the contact page.

    Why is your website so minimal?

    We focus our energy on building systems, not websites. The site reflects who we are: direct, deliberate, and substance over style.
