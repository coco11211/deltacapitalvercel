Methodology

Model Validation

Our research process is predicated on a commitment to out-of-sample validation and the prevention of overfitting. Models are subjected to a battery of tests, including walk-forward validation and Monte Carlo stress testing, to ensure their robustness across a wide range of potential market regimes.

A model is not considered for production unless it demonstrates statistical significance after accounting for transaction costs, execution slippage, and data-mining bias.

Ensemble Approach

We do not rely on a single monolithic model. Instead, we deploy an ensemble of diverse, uncorrelated predictive models. This approach reduces model-specific risk and creates a more resilient portfolio. Signals are generated from a variety of sources and combined using a proprietary weighting algorithm that considers both the model's historical performance and its recent predictive accuracy.
