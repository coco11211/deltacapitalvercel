Risk & Controls

Our risk management philosophy is predicated on the principle of capital preservation. We employ a sophisticated framework of real-time, automated controls to manage market, liquidity, and operational risks. Our systems are designed to identify and mitigate potential threats before they can impact the portfolio.

We utilize a variety of quantitative techniques, including Value-at-Risk (VaR) and Conditional Value-at-Risk (CVaR), to model and manage portfolio risk. Stress testing and scenario analysis are regularly conducted to assess the portfolio's resilience to extreme market events.
