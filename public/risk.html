Risk & Controls

Systematic Risk Management

Risk is not an afterthought; it is a core component of our investment process. We employ a multi-layered framework of pre-trade, real-time, and post-trade risk controls. Portfolio-level constraints are systematically enforced, and exposures are monitored continuously against predefined limits.

Measurement & Mitigation

Our risk measurement system utilizes a combination of Value-at-Risk (VaR), Conditional Value-at-Risk (CVaR), and proprietary stress tests. These metrics inform portfolio construction and tactical adjustments. We explicitly model and manage liquidity risk, execution risk, and model decay.

Operational Integrity

The firm maintains a clear separation between research, portfolio management, and execution. All trading activity is automated and monitored through a system of checks and balances designed to ensure operational integrity and minimize the potential for human error. Access to critical systems is strictly controlled and logged.
