System Architecture

Our infrastructure is engineered for resilience, scale, and low-latency performance. We view our technology not merely as a support function, but as a core pillar of our competitive advantage. System Principles

Modularity: Each component of our system is designed as a discrete, independently-verifiable service, allowing for rapid development and robust testing.

Redundancy: Critical systems have multiple layers of redundancy to ensure high availability and fault tolerance, from data feeds to execution gateways.

Security: We employ a defense-in-depth security posture, with multiple layers of access control, encryption, and continuous monitoring to protect our intellectual property and operational integrity.
