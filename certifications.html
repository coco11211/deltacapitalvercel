    Regulatory

    SEC Registration

    Delta Capital is registered with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) as an investment adviser. Our registration allows us to provide investment advisory services to qualified investors.

    Our SEC registration demonstrates our commitment to regulatory compliance and adherence to the Investment Advisers Act of 1940. All staff members comply with applicable licensing and continuing education requirements.

    Standards

    Industry Standards & Commitments

    Beyond regulatory compliance, Delta Capital adheres to industry best practices and professional standards.

    • GIPS Standards — Our performance reporting follows Global Investment Performance Standards to ensure transparency and comparability.
    • SOC 2 Compliance — Our operational controls and security practices are designed to meet SOC 2 Type II standards for systems reliability and data protection.
    • Cybersecurity Protocols — We implement industry-standard cybersecurity practices to protect client information and operational integrity.
    • Business Continuity — Our operations maintain disaster recovery and business continuity plans to ensure uninterrupted service delivery.
    Governance

    Code of Ethics & Conduct

    All Delta Capital team members operate under a comprehensive code of ethics that emphasizes fiduciary responsibility, conflicts of interest management, and professional integrity. Our ethical framework governs trading activities, client interactions, and internal decision-making processes.

    We maintain strict policies regarding insider trading, market manipulation, and misuse of client information. All team members receive regular training on ethical obligations and regulatory requirements.
