    Compliance & Standards

    As we build Delta Capital, we are designing our operations with regulatory compliance and institutional standards in mind from the beginning — not as an afterthought.

    Regulatory

    Regulatory Planning

    We are building our infrastructure and compliance processes with the intention of meeting applicable regulatory requirements as we scale. This includes understanding the registration and reporting obligations that will apply as we move toward managing capital.

    Standards

    Standards We Are Building Toward

    We are designing our systems and processes to align with industry best practices from the start.

    • Cybersecurity — Industry-standard security practices are being implemented to protect data and operational integrity.
    • Auditability — Every system component is being designed with logging, reproducibility, and audit trail capabilities.
    • Business Continuity — Disaster recovery and resilience planning are part of our architecture from day one.
    Governance

    Ethics & Conduct

    Integrity is a design constraint, not a marketing claim. We are building a culture of transparency, intellectual honesty, and accountability. These values inform how we write code, conduct research, and make decisions.
