    Components

    Pipeline Architecture

    01 — Data Ingestion

    Multi-source aggregation via WebSocket and REST connections. Real-time and historical data pipelines with integrity validation and caching layers.

    02 — Feature Engineering

    Raw market data is transformed into a structured feature space. Technical indicators, statistical measures, and derived signals are computed with strict versioning.

    03 — Model Ensemble

    Multiple model families generate predictions independently. An ensemble layer combines outputs using performance-weighted aggregation with regime-conditional adjustments.

    04 — Risk Engine

    Real-time risk computation across positions, strategies, and the aggregate portfolio. Limit enforcement will be programmatic and operate without manual override during trading sessions.

    05 — Portfolio Optimizer

    Constrained optimization incorporating risk budgets, transaction costs, and market impact estimates. Designed to output target weights that balance conviction with implementability.

    06 — Execution Layer

    Order management with smart routing, fill tracking, and slippage monitoring. Broker integration will support both paper and live trading environments.

    07 — Monitoring & Compliance

    System health monitoring, compliance enforcement, and automated audit trail generation. Every state change will be recorded immutably.

    Design Principles

    Deterministic

    Given identical inputs, every component will produce identical outputs. Reproducibility is a design invariant.

    Observable

    Every computation will be logged, measurable, and auditable. The system will not operate in shadows.

    Resilient

    Component failure will trigger graceful degradation, not cascade failure. Every layer is designed to fail safely.
